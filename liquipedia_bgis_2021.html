    Battlegrounds Mobile India Series 2021 or BGIS 2021 is the first edition of Battlegrounds Mobile India Series (BGIS). The tournament has been organized by KRAFTON and is sponsored by iQOO and features a total prize pool of ₹1,00,00,000 INR.

    Format[edit]

    Battlegrounds Mobile India Series 2021: 4th December 2021 to 16th January 2022

    Maps: TPP Erangel, Miramar and Sanhok

    • In-game Qualifiers: 4th December 2021 to 8th December 2021
      • Open for all
      • In-game qualifiers will be held to determine the contenders for the online qualifiers.
      • Every registered squad will be required to play 15 matches, out of which 10 best games will be taken into consideration for qualification.
      • Top 1024 teams advance to Online-Qualifiers
    • Online-Qualifiers: 16th December 2021 to 30th December 2021
      • 1024 Teams
      • The Online-Qualifiers qualifiers have been divided into three rounds (Round 1, 2 and 3)
      • 32 teams will be invited for Round 3.
      • 2 matches will be played in Round 1 & 2 and multiple teams will be eliminated with each round.
      • 3 matches will be played in Round 3 and multiple teams will be eliminated.
      • 64 teams advance to Quarter-Finals
    • Quarter-Finals: 2nd January 2022 to 5th January 2022
      • 64 Teams
      • These 64 teams will be divided into 4 groups
      • Each group will play a total of 6 matches
      • Top 6 teams from each group will qualify for the Semi-Finals.
      • 24 teams advance to Semi-Finals
    • Semi-Finals: 7th January 2022 to 10th January 2022
      • 24 Teams
      • These 24 teams will be divided into 3 groups
      • The groups will face off against each other in a Round-Robin format.
      • Each group will play a total of 8 Round Robins.
      • 16 teams advance to Grand-Finals
    • Grand Finals: 13th January 2022 to 16th January 2022
      • 16 Teams
      • 24 matches to be played over a period of 4 days to determine the winner.

    Allocations[edit]

    • Winner qualified for PMGC 2021 - Grand Finals

    *In order to compete in the PMGC 2021 - Grand Finals, teams needed to submit travel documents by January 16th, 2022.
    As a result, GodLike Esports secures the spot as the team with the highest rank and holds all the travel requirements.     [1]
